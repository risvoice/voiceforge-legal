About VoiceForge Reader

VoiceForge Reader reads pasted, selected, or shared text aloud on iPhone and iPad. You can use the main app to paste text and tap Speak, or use the Share Extension from apps such as Notes and Safari.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does VoiceForge Reader require an account?

No. There are no accounts, logins, subscriptions, or user profiles.

Does speech work offline?

No. VoiceForge Reader needs an internet connection because speech is generated through an external text-to-speech endpoint.

Why does playback stop when I close the Share Sheet?

In the current beta, Share Extension playback runs inside the extension UI lifetime. Keep the Share Sheet open if you expect continued reading.

What text can I read?

You can paste text in the main app or share selected text, plain text, URLs, and readable page content through the iOS Share Sheet.

How is my text handled?

The text you ask the app to read is sent to an external Edge/Bing text-to-speech endpoint to generate audio. The developer does not operate that endpoint and does not receive your text. Please read the Privacy Policy before reading sensitive content.
